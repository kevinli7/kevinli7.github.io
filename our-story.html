Our Story

The journey that brought us together

How We Met

In the Fall of 2019, Kevin and Sabrina met on Hinge, where a picture of Kevin in a pink apron caught Sabrina's eye

They talked a lot and kept discovering more and more things in common (such as having the same drawing tablet) and eventually went on their first date at Santana Row (RIP Amazon Books).

Early Dating

Things were going well, but then the Covid pandemic happened and we went a few months without seeing each other. This period was tough, but solidifed our relationship through long hours of playing Stardew Valley. and Animal Crossing virtually together.

Eventually, life became normal again and we were able go on many memorable dates and trips together.

The Proposal

One of our favorite weekend getaway locations is Monterey, having gone multiple times throughout the years.

In October of 2024, Kevin planned a *casual* trip to Monterey. During the trip, we enjoyed the ocean vibes and the aquarium before Kevin proposed to Sabrina at the Spa. Kevin hid the ring inside a Sonny Angel box and we have a unboxing video to prove it (ask Sabrina if you want to see it).

Our Future Together

We're so excited to start our lives together and celebrate with all of you!

We're not quite sure what the future holds, but we're excited to find out together.
